Experience

This past summer and in Fall 2019, I interned with NASA Goddard Space Flight Center with the Avionics and Electrical Ground Support Equipment Group as a Pathways Intern working on the Roman Space Telescope. I developed software and hardware to test the deployment system on the RST.

In Summer 2019, I interned with Lockheed Martin's Rotary and Missions Systems Division MASC-F group as a UI/UX intern and participated in the Mission Concept Academy as Deputy Project Manager with NASA's L'SPACE program, working with other students to propose a mission concept and write a preliminary design review. You can view our team website and PDR here!

I have work experience with The Aerospace Corporation, working on a proprietary visualization engine and other projects as a Software Engineering Intern. I've optimized applications with regards to UI/UX on these projects.

Outside of school and work, I'm constantly a part of the Yellow Jacket Space Program's Avionics Team creating circuit boards and writing software to interface with a microcontroller. Our team goal is to send a liquid engine rocket into space.

I also perform research as co-lead of the Avionics with Space Systems Design Lab at Georgia Tech with Dr. Glenn Lightsey, working on GT-2, a 1U cubesat. I've also worked on designing a database for our Mission Control Center and various projects for STRATOS, a high altitude balloon project.
